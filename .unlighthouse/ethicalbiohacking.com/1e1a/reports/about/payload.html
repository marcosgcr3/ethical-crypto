Our Editorial Process

As a health and wellness publication (classified as YMYL — Your Money Your Life), we hold ourselves to the highest medical integrity standards. Every piece of content on Ethical Biohacking follows a rigorous multi-step process:

1

Research & Literature Review

Every article begins with a comprehensive review of peer-reviewed studies sourced from PubMed, Google Scholar, and established medical journals. We prioritize meta-analyses and randomized controlled trials (RCTs) over observational studies or anecdotal evidence.

2

Expert Writing & Self-Experimentation

Content is authored by experienced clinicians and biohackers who have personally tested the protocols discussed. Where applicable, we include our own data and results alongside the published research, providing a real-world lens on clinical findings.

3

Clinical Review & Fact-Checking

All health claims are cross-referenced against current clinical literature. Dosages, contraindications, and safety warnings are verified against authoritative sources (NIH, WHO, Mayo Clinic). Any claim that cannot be substantiated is removed or clearly labeled as preliminary.

4

Ongoing Updates & Corrections

Science evolves. We revisit published articles regularly to incorporate new research findings, update recommendations, and correct any information that has been superseded. Every article displays its publication and last-updated dates for full transparency.

Corrections Policy: If you identify an error, outdated claim, or misrepresented study in any of our content, please contact us immediately. We are committed to issuing corrections transparently and promptly. Our ego is subordinate to scientific accuracy.
