1. THE BIOLOGICAL INTERNET: UNDERSTANDING VAGAL TONE

However, the most critical discovery of recent years is the Afferent-Efferent Imbalance: roughly 80% of these fibers are sensory (afferent), carrying signals from the gut environment up to the brainstem.
