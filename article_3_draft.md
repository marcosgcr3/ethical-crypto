        <p class="opacity-80 leading-relaxed m-0 mb-6">
            Both Berberine and Metformin activate adenosine monophosphate-activated protein kinase (AMPK). This pathway stimulates glucose uptake in muscles and improves insulin sensitivity. However, Berberine offers a "dual-action" benefit by also acting as a mild lipid-lowering agent, which can be critical for biohackers managing cardiovascular risks alongside glucose.
        </p>
        <p class="opacity-80 leading-relaxed m-0">
            Current research suggests that while Metformin is more predictable in its pharmacokinetic profile, Berberine's secondary metabolites interact more favorably with the gut microbiome, promoting the growth of beneficial bacteria like *Akkermansia muciniphila*.
        </p>
