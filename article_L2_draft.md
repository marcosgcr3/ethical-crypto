        <p class="opacity-80 leading-relaxed m-0 mb-6">
            Epigenetic clocks track DNA methylation: the biochemical switches that determine which genes are active. Originally mapped out by Steve Horvath, these patterns accurately correlate with organismal aging across mammals.
        </p>
        <p class="opacity-80 leading-relaxed m-0">
            However, consumer clocks frequently suffer from "test-retest reliability" issues. The environment, stress, and recent diets can swing results wildly, blurring the line between systemic age reversal and acute physiological fluctuations.
        </p>
